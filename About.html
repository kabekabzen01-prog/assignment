Our Story

Mo'PLK Events Co. - short for Mo Polokwane - was founded by Trevor Kabe in 2023, but honestly, it started long before that. Growing up in Turfloop, Trevor and his friends would help neighbours plan and pull off local celebrations — birthdays, family gatherings, you name it. What began as something fun between friends slowly turned into something bigger.

With every event, the team sharpened their skills, built real connections in the community, and developed a genuine love for making occasions memorable. Word spread quickly, and before long Mo'PLK Events Co. had grown into a trusted name across Polokwane and beyond. Today, the business specialises in catering for traditional ceremonies, weddings, baby showers, graduations, corporate events, and other meaningful milestones. Every plate is prepared with care, every detail is considered, and every client is treated like family.
