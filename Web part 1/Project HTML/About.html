History

Mo'PLK Event Co. (Mo Polokwane) is a event planning business founded by Trevor Kabe in 2023. What started as a simple side hustle in the streets of Turfloop—where me and my friends would help neighbours plan and manage their local events which grew into a trusted business in the community. With each event, our hands-on experience and real passion for creating beautiful, memorable events helped us perfect our reputation. Today, Mo'PLK Event Co. specialises in planning and coordinating meaningful celebrations such as traditional ceremonies, baby showers, graduation parties, weddings, and other special family occasions. Over the years, the business has earned a strong reputation through word-of-mouth recommendations and the stunning photos of our events shared on social media. Now, Mo'PLK Event Co. continues to bring warmth, professionalism, and a deep respect for cultural traditions to every celebration they touch.
