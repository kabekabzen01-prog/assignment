Our Policies

Read terms and Conditions below

Costs

All catering quotes are provided based on the number of guests, menu selection, and event type. A non-refundable deposit of 30% of the total quote is required to confirm your booking. The remaining balance is due no later than 7 days before the event date. Prices are subject to change based on ingredient availability and travel distance to the venue. Any additional guests or menu changes requested after confirmation may result in adjusted costs.

Validity

All quotes issued by Mo'PLK Events Co. are valid for 14 days from the date of issue. After this period, prices may be revised due to changes in supplier costs or availability. A booking is only considered confirmed once the deposit has been received and a confirmation email has been sent to the client. Mo'PLK Events Co. reserves the right to decline a booking at its discretion.

Cancellation

Should you need to cancel your booking, please notify us in writing as soon as possible. Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event will forfeit the deposit only. Cancellations made within 7 to 14 days of the event will be charged 50% of the total booking amount. Cancellations made less than 7 days before the event will be charged the full amount. Mo'PLK Events Co. reserves the right to cancel a booking due to unforeseen circumstances, in which case a full refund will be issued to the client.
