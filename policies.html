Our Policies

Read terms and Conditions below

Our catering quotes are always based on the number of guests, the menu you select, and the type of event you're planning. To secure your booking, we require a 30% non-refundable deposit upfront. The rest of the balance needs to be settled at least 7 days before the event.

Just a heads-up that prices can sometimes change depending on ingredient availability and how far we have to travel to your venue. Any extra guests or menu adjustments made after we have confirmed the booking may also affect the final cost.

All quotes from Mo'PLK Events Co. are valid for 14 days from the date we send them. After that, prices may need to be adjusted if supplier costs or availability change. Your booking is only officially confirmed once we have received the deposit and sent you a confirmation email. Please note that we reserve the right to decline any booking if needed.

If you need to cancel your booking, please let us know in writing as soon as possible.

Here is how our cancellation policy works:

  • Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event will only forfeit the deposit.
  • Cancellations made between 7 and 14 days before the event will be charged 50% of the total booking amount.
  • Cancellations made less than 7 days before the event will be charged the full amount.

Please note that Mo'PLK Events Co. reserves the right to cancel a booking due to unforeseen circumstances, in which case we will issue a full refund.
